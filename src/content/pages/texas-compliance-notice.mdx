Consumers wishing to file a complaint against a company or a residential mortgage loan originator should complete and send a complaint form to:


<address>

Texas Department of Savings and Mortgage Lending <br/>
2601 North Lamar,<br/>
Suite 201, Austin, Texas 78705.<br/>

</address>

Complaint forms and instructions may be obtained from the department’s website at www.sml.texas.gov. 

A toll-free consumer hotline is available at 1-877-276-5550.

The department maintains a recovery fund to make payments of certain actual out of pocket damages sustained by borrowers caused by acts of licensed residential mortgage loan originators.

A written application for reimbursement from the recovery fund must be filed with and investigated by the department prior to the payment of a claim. for more information about the recovery fund, please consult the department’s website at www.sml.texas.gov.
